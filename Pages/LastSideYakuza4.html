Yakuza 4

General info / "Synopsis"

Takes place and was released in 2010 and it follows four main characters: Shun Akiyama, Taiga Saejima Masayoshi Tanimura and Kazuma Kiryu. The story follows them through their own stories which by the end of their part gets connected by a single girl. All of this leads to them discovering multiple conspiracies that they have/want to fix.
