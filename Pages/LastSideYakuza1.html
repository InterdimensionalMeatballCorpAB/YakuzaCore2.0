Yakuza

General info / "Synopsis"

The first ever Yakuza game, released in 2005 it features Kazuma Kiryu as the main character who after getting out of prison discovers and dismantles a conspiracy within his Yakuza organization, the Tojo clan. He does this while also helping a small child by the name of Haruka find her mother, which gets him involved in trouble.
