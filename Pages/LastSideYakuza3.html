Yakuza 3

General info / "Synopsis"

Originally released in 2009 it had a remaster that released worldwide in 2019 with the same name, both star Kazuma Kiryu as their main characters. Kiryu has now moved down to Okinawa and is handeling an orphanage, all the while the tojo clan is mixed into yet another conspiracy that this time may threaten Kiryus livelyhood.
