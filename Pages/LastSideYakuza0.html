Yakuza 0

General Info

Released in 2015, it stars the main characters Kazuma Kiryu and Goro Majima during the late 80s Japan in the peak of the housing bubble while showing the backstories of both these iconic characters

Director's cut

The director's cut is a remaster of Yakuza 0 with added cutscenes and a multiplayer gamemode. Unfortunately these added cutscenes retcon a couple things, and many agree that this "ruins" the story. Sadly there is no way to buy the original version on steam anymore.
