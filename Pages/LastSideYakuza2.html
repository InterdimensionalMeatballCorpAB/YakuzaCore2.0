Yakuza 2

General info / "Synopsis"

Yakuza 2, released in 2006 features Kazuma Kiryu as the Main character. Kiryu helps the tojo clan with meeting the Omi Allience to stabilize their relations, but this is stopped by many new conspiracies and grudges from the past, while this happens Kiryu is involved in a romantic plotline with a certain someone.
